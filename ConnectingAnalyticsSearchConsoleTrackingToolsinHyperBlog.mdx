title: "Connecting Analytics, Search Console & Tracking Tools in HyperBlog"
---

HyperBlog makes it simple to integrate essential tools like **Google Analytics (GA4)**, **Google Search Console**, **chat widgets**, **tracking pixels**, and **custom scripts** — all without needing a developer.

### **Where to Set It Up**

Go to your **HyperBlog Dashboard** → **Settings** → **SEO Settings**. Here, you’ll find all the integration options neatly organized.

![SEO Settings Pn](/images/SEO-settings.png)

### **Google Analytics (GA4)**

- Add your **Google Analytics ID** to track your site’s traffic and performance.
- Once entered, HyperBlog will automatically trigger a **cookie consent popup** for GDPR compliance.

### **Google Search Console**

1. Go to Google Search Console.
2. Add a new property (your domain or URL).
3. Choose **HTML tag verification method**.

Copy the \<meta\> tag provided by Google (something like this):\
\<meta name="google-site-verification" content="your-verification-code" /\>

4. Go to **HyperBlog → Settings → SEO Settings → Head Scripts**.
5. Paste the entire \<meta\> tag there.
6. Click **Save** and go back to Google Search Console to **Verify**.

Once verified, your ownership will be confirmed, and your site will be eligible for indexing and performance reporting.

### **Adding Chat, Pixels & Custom Tracking Scripts**

You can easily embed third-party tools like:

- [**Tawk.to**](http://Tawk.to)** / Crisp chat**
- **Meta Pixel (Facebook)**
- **LinkedIn Insight Tag**
- **Google Tag Manager**
- Or any other JS-based tool

Use these fields:

- **Miscellaneous Scripts** – Adds code **site-wide**, ideal for chat widgets or pixel codes.
- **Head Scripts** – Inserts scripts directly in the \<head\>, perfect for SEO or tracking verification.

![Seo Settings Add Css Pn](/images/seo-settings-add-css.png)

### **Other SEO Settings You Can Configure**

- **Google AdSense Publisher ID**: (for Auto Ads only)
- **AdSense ads.txt**: Paste your required ads.txt file here.
- **Enable Default Cookie Popup**: Automatically display a GDPR-compliant cookie banner.
- **Privacy Policy URL**: Link users to your privacy policy from the cookie popup.

With HyperBlog, you don’t need to modify theme files or use third-party plugins to add analytics or tracking tools. Just go to your **SEO Settings**, paste your code, and it’s live. It’s fast, flexible, and built for creators who want full control without technical hassle.
