---
title: "Managing Categories & Tags in Hyperblog"
---

Organize your blog content for better structure, easier navigation, and stronger SEO. Hyperblog makes it effortless to create and manage both **categories** and **tags** all from a single dashboard, with no need for plugins or external tools.

## Categories

To create a new category:

1. Go to the **Categories** section in your Hyperblog dashboard.
2. Click **Add New Category**.

![Addnewcategory Pn](/images/addnewcategory.png)

3. Enter your category name in the popup and click **Submit**.

![Submitcategory Pn](/images/submitcategory.png)

4. Your category will be **instantly created**.

![Categorycreatedsuccessfully Pn](/images/categorycreatedsuccessfully.png)

You can create **unlimited categories** based on your content needs. You can also **edit** or **delete** any category at any time using the simple interface.

### To assign a category to a blog post:

- Open the post in the editor.
- Click on **Post Info**.

![Postinfotoassigncategory Pn](/images/postinfotoassigncategory.png)

- Under **Category**, select the one you want to assign from the dropdown.

![Assigncategory Pn](/images/assigncategory.png)

## Tags

### Managing tags works the same way:

1. Go to the **Tags** section in the dashboard.
2. Click **Add New Tag**.

![Createtags Pn](/images/createtags.png)

3. Enter your tag name in the popup and click **Submit**.

![Submittag Pn](/images/submittag.png)

4. Your tag will be **instantly created**.

![Tagscreatedsuccessfully Pn](/images/tagscreatedsuccessfully.png)

Just like categories, you can create **unlimited tags** and manage them anytime including **editing** or **deleting** when needed.

### To assign tags to a post:

- In the post editor, go to **Post Info**.

![Postinfotoassigntags Pn](/images/postinfotoassigntags.png)

- Under **Tags**, select your preferred tags.

![Assigntag Pn](/images/assigntag.png)

Using categories and tags in Hyperblog helps you keep your content organized, improves navigation, and boosts SEO. With an intuitive interface and unlimited options, managing them is simple and efficient. Start creating your own structure and make your blog easier to explore for readers and search engines.
