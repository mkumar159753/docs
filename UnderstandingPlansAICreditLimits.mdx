## Manage Your HyperBlog Subscription with Ease

HyperBlog offers flexible plans to suit everyone—from solo creators to growing teams and enterprises. Here’s a complete overview of available plans, what they include, and how to manage upgrades, downgrades, or cancellations.

## Available Plans

Perfect for getting started with the basics.

## Upgrading, Downgrading, or Cancelling Your Plan

HyperBlog makes it easy to manage your subscription:

### Upgrade

1. Go to **Settings → Upgrade Plan**
2. Choose a higher-tier plan
3. Confirm upgrade changes take effect immediately

### **Downgrade**

1. Visit **Settings → Upgrade Plan**
2. Select a lower-tier plan
3. Downgrade will apply from your next billing cycle

### **Cancel Subscription** 

- Your plan will remain active until the current billing period ends
- You can still access your content after cancellation (in read-only or exportable mode)

Choose a plan that matches your blogging goals from trying HyperBlog for free, to scaling content with AI. Plan upgrades and downgrades are smooth, and soon you'll be able to track AI credit usage directly from your dashboard.
